
# Review Genie

An AI-powered review generator that helps users craft perfectly toned and detailed reviews for any business or place. Whether you're praising a hidden gem or providing constructive feedback to a local establishment, Review Genie ensures your message is clear, professional, and impactful.

## Features

- **Custom Rating**: Select from 1 to 5 stars to set the initial sentiment.
- **Metric-Based Feedback**: Fine-tune your review with specific categories like Quality, Service, Cleanliness, and Accessibility.
- **Adjustable Tones**: Choose from various tones including Professional, Enthusiastic, Casual, and Critical to match your style.
- **Contextual Details**: Add specific notes about your visit to make the AI-generated review more authentic.
- **One-Click Actions**: Copy the generated review or post it directly to your favorite platforms.
- **Session History**: Keep track of your recently generated reviews.

## How to Use

1. **Enter Business Name**: Tell the Genie where you visited.
2. **Set the Rating**: Choose your overall satisfaction level.
3. **Select Metrics**: Click the icons to mark aspects as "Great," "Poor," or "N/A."
4. **Choose a Tone**: Pick the vibe that fits your experience.
5. **Generate**: Hit the button and let the magic happen!
6. **Copy & Share**: Use the copy button to save the text to your clipboard.

## Technology

Built with React, Tailwind CSS, and Lucide Icons, powered by advanced language processing to create human-like responses.
